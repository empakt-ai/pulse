Features

The intelligence layer over your social.

PULSE is a single workspace that fetches your posts, watches your competitors, and ships a daily brief that tells you the one thing you should do today — backed by your real numbers.

Built around the morning brief.

Every weekday at 6 AM in your local time zone, PULSE generates a one-screen brief: a verdict, six prioritised actions, and the signals behind both. No dashboards to interpret. No 30 graphs. One read.

① Verdict

The one-sentence read on your week

An AI-generated summary that cites specific posts, specific numbers, and the strategic implication. Not "engagement is up" — "your Friday DTC reel is doing 11× your IG baseline, push the format on TikTok this week."

② Action plan

Six moves, prioritised by urgency

Each action references a real post or competitor, fits a specific time bucket (Now / Today / This week / This month), and includes the metric it's expected to move. Cut directly into your Monday standup.

③ Signal stream

Pattern detection across your catalogue

Viral peaks, missed cross-platform opportunities, series arcs, collaboration multipliers, engagement velocity, language-split anomalies — PULSE detects 14 distinct signal kinds and surfaces the ones that matter today.

④ Reach scorecard

You vs the brands you track

Horizontal "% of leader" bars combining your accounts with every tracked competitor. The user's row is highlighted in brand color so the gap is obvious at a glance.

Seven platforms. One feed.

PULSE reads from the official APIs of every platform — never your password, never a scraping workaround that breaks every month.

Instagram

Posts, reels, stories, comments, follower counts. Business / Creator accounts only.

TikTok

Videos, plays, shares, comments. Business or Creator accounts. Captions read in any language.

YouTube

Long-form and Shorts. Direct integration via Google Data API. Includes view-through and CTR.

LinkedIn

Company-page posts plus personal-brand creator content. Comment threading included.

Facebook

Pages, video posts, story content. Cross-posted reach merges with Instagram automatically.

X (Twitter)

Posts, replies, impressions, engagement rate. Pulls your last 30 days every sync.

Snapchat

Snap views, story completion, audience demographics. GCC-strong region intelligence.

Competitor tracking

Add up to 50 public handles. PULSE runs the same analysis on them and feeds the gap into your brief.

Cross-platform content intelligence.

PULSE groups your cross-posts into content pieces so the system understands a Reel and its TikTok counterpart are the same idea. It detects series automatically ("Part 1, Part 2, …"), measures language splits in your captions, and benchmarks your hooks against your top competitors — surfacing exactly which format to copy and which to retire.

Reports, exports, and the weekly digest.

Generate a one-page executive PDF on demand from any workspace. History stays in your account. Opt into the weekly Sunday digest and PULSE emails the fresh brief — with the PDF attached — to you or a shared agency inbox every Sunday morning at 6 AM local.
