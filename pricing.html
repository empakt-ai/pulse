Frequently asked.

How does the 14-day free trial work?

Sign up, connect at least one social account, and PULSE delivers the full feature set for 14 days — no credit card required. We email you on day 11 to remind you the trial is winding down. After day 14 your data stays put; you just need to start a plan to keep generating new briefs.

Are prices per workspace or per user?

Per workspace. A "workspace" is one brand or one creator identity with its own connected accounts and competitor list. If you manage three brands, you'd have three workspaces — each with its own plan. Agency-tier seat sharing (one bill, many workspaces) is on the Q3 roadmap.

What payment methods do you accept?

Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) through Stripe. For accounts in Saudi Arabia and the GCC we accept mada cards. Agency-plan annual billing via invoice is available — write to contact.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from Settings → Billing. You keep access through the end of your billing period; no phone calls, no retention specialist, no friction. Your data exports to JSON on request before deletion.

Do you offer a discount for annual billing?

Yes — pay annually and you get two months free across every plan. The annual toggle ships with the billing module shortly after launch; until then we'll apply the discount manually on Agency-plan invoices.

Is my data safe?

PULSE never sees your social platform passwords — every connection is via the platform's official OAuth flow. Your data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL with Row Level Security) hosted on AWS, with daily backups. We are GDPR-compliant for EU customers and PIPEDA-compliant for Canadian customers. Full details on the privacy page.

Can I upgrade or downgrade mid-cycle?

Yes. Upgrades are prorated immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period so you don't lose access to features you've already paid for.
